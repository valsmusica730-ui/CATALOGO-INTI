Our Journey

A bridge between two worlds

Founded by Fernanda, an American mother with Ecuadorian roots, INTI SOMOS emerged from a powerful pursuit: finding truly nourishing food for her family—without compromising taste, quality, or purpose.

In Ecuador, she rediscovered ancestral ingredients like chocho—a nutrient-dense Andean superfood rich in protein and fiber—alongside natural foods cultivated with deep respect for the land. What she found was more than nutrition; it was a living heritage of wellness.

INTI SOMOS—“Sun” and “We Are”—represents life, energy, and connection. It is a celebration of who we are when we nourish ourselves with intention, bringing conscious nutrition to a global community.
