Our Story

A bridge between
two worlds

Founded by Fernanda, an American mother with Ecuadorian roots, INTI SOMOS emerged from a powerful pursuit: finding truly nourishing food for her family—without compromising taste or quality.

In Ecuador, she rediscovered ancestral ingredients like chocho, a nutrient-dense Andean superfood. What she found was more than nutrition; it was a living heritage of wellness.

INTI SOMOS—“Sun” and “We Are”—represents life and connection. It is a celebration of who we are when we nourish ourselves with intention.
