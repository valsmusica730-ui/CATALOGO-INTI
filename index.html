Founded by Fernanda, an American mother with Ecuadorian roots, the brand emerged from a simple yet powerful pursuit: finding truly nourishing food for her family—without compromising taste, quality, or purpose.

In Ecuador, she rediscovered ancestral ingredients like chocho—a nutrient-dense Andean superfood rich in protein and fiber—alongside vibrant fruits and natural foods cultivated with deep respect for the land.

Back in the United States, that discovery became a vision: to share the strength, purity, and vitality of Andean nutrition with families around the world.

INTI SOMOS—“Sun” and “We Are”—represents life, energy, and connection. It is a celebration of who we are when we nourish ourselves with intention.

More than food, INTI SOMOS is a bridge between ancestral wisdom and modern living—bringing conscious nutrition to a global community.
